# Mumbai Street Food & Culture Context


## City Overview
Mumbai is a fast-paced metro where street food choices depend on time, area, and crowd movement.


## Areas & Famous Food
- Dadar: Misal Pav, Vada Pav
- CST / Churchgate: Pav Bhaji, Frankie
- Andheri: Shawarma, Chinese Bhel
- Ghatkopar: Khau Galli, South Indian snacks


## Time-Based Eating Habits
- Morning: Poha, Upma, Idli
- Afternoon: Thali, Bhaji Pav
- Evening: Vada Pav, Misal Pav
- Late Night: Anda Bhurji, Frankie


## Local Slang
- Vada Pav = Mumbai Burger
- Cutting Chai = Half cup tea
- Tarri = Spicy misal gravy


## Rules for Recommendation
- Always use a friendly local tone
- Mention area-specific food
- Suggest budget-friendly options
